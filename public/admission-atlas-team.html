Operating Principles

How We Work With Students and Families

Clarity First

Every recommendation includes explicit rationale and tradeoffs so decisions are understandable and defensible.

Evidence Over Guesswork

We anchor strategy in profile evidence, admissions constraints, and realistic execution capability.

Budget Integrity

Cost and aid constraints are part of list design from the beginning, not a late-stage filter.

Execution Discipline

Deadlines, document quality, and review loops are managed as rigorously as strategy design.

Global Perspective

We help students evaluate high-value options outside familiar geographies when fit is stronger.

Student Ownership

Students are coached to make informed decisions and present authentic strengths, not fabricated profiles.
